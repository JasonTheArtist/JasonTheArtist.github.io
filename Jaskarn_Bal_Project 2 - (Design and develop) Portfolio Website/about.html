About Me

Heya! I'm Jaskarn Bal, but I go by Jason. Here's a little background about me: I'm a 23-year-old man, born and raised in Canada, with Indian heritage. When it comes to my hobbies, I enjoy illustration—primarily digital illustration—and graphic design. I also love playing video games and exploring creative writing.

Currently, I'm a college student at George Brown College, studying Graphic Design. I started the program back in Fall 2022, and since then, I’ve learned a lot and built an extensive portfolio. My work ranges from logo design to editorial design and much more.
